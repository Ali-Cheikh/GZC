About Us

This club is dedicated to offering both educational and competitive activities for students at ISET Sfax. Through tournaments and workshops in the fields of e-sports and traditional sports, we aim to foster a vibrant community. Our inspiration for founding this club stems from the growing popularity of electronic and athletic gaming, particularly in Tunisia, and the increasing interest from major companies in these fields.

Discovering and Developing Talent

One of our main goals is to identify and nurture the talents within our institution. By providing support and development opportunities, we aim to create a competitive foundation that encourages growth and excellence across institutions.

Engaging Students in Gaming and Game Development

We seek to actively involve students in the world of video gaming. By offering a broad overview of the gaming industry, we aim to spark interest in game development and all related fields, providing students with a pathway into this exciting area of technology.

Encouraging Interaction through Competitive Tournaments

Our club promotes student interaction and camaraderie by organizing competitive tournaments. These events allow students to test their skills, collaborate, and build a supportive community around shared interests.

Expanding Activities Nationally

We aim to elevate our activities to a national level. By building partnerships with companies interested in the gaming and sports industries, we hope to attract new contributions and create lasting agreements that benefit both students and industry stakeholders.
